About Us

Who we are

Our company is a oil press machine supplier, we has committed to manufacture oil expeller press for 40 years.

Built in 1981,we start to manufacture oil press machines and automatic oil mill production lines used for oil mill plants.

After 40 years development, up to now, we obtained fairly strong production capability and competent to provide a complete edible oil extraction solutions, for small business workshops, medium and large-scaled oil milling plants projects.
