03.

Oilseed Flaking Machine

With the rolling pressure of flaking machine, oilseed materials can be turned from granular particles into 0.3millimeter flakes.

The purpose is to obtain the maximum oil extraction yield rate, by increasing the surface contact area. After oilseed flaking process, seed oil residues only remains a low rate of 1 percent.

It’s designed for most of the types of oilseeds, capacity range 30,50,70,100,120,150 up to 200 tons per day. Flakes thickness (mm) :soybean <0.3; rapeseed <0.35; peanut <0.5 ;sunflower seed 0.3-0.5; cotton seed <0.4

03.

Oilseed Steaming/ Roasting Machine

The function of oilseed roasting machines is to humidify the oilseed, and then spray steam by heating and frying dry at the end.

Oilseed steaming process is a kind of ripening oilseed embryo process, aims to soften oilseed flakes to be cooked billet.It helps to improve oil press quality and increase seed oil yield rate.

After steaming, the mature oilseed material is completely ready to oil press processing. This machine can work continuously, with features of large capacity up to 300~400 tons per day, low power consumption, easy to adjust steaming parameter, easy to opertion and mainteniance.

Edible Oil Extraction Machine Sets

Edible Oil Refining Processing Machines

01.

Edible Oil Refinery Machines

After oilseed pressing/pre-pressing and/or extraction processing, “crude oil” has been produced with higher content impurities,not pure enough to reach edible oil standard, further refining process is required to clear away the unwanted stuff.

The main refinery secions includes:

  1. Degumming (by hydration settlement method generally)
  2. Deacidification (by either physical or chemical method)
  3. Decolorization (add white clay as adsorbent)
  4. Deodorization (by distillation method)
  5. Dewaxing (optional,only for higher wax content oilseeds e.g.sunflower, corn germ, safflower seed,rice bran)
